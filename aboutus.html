Julia Langwade

BSc(hons) Nursing studies
BSc(hons) MChs Podiatry

Julia is our lead clinician and studied at the University of Salford graduating with first class honours in Podiatry. She is HCPC registered and a member of the society of Chiropodists and Podiatrists.
Julia is also a registered nurse with 38 years experience in healthcare with extensive experience in surgical, vascular and dementia care
Julia's healthcare background also includes vast management experience, in particular regarding Infection Prevention, clinical governance and health and safety in a care setting
